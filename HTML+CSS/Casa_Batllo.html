Services

GIFT SHOP

In the museum shop at Casa Batlló you will find a wide range of artisanal products related to Gaudí and Barcelona, such as souvenirs, books and jewelry

FREE WI-FI

We offer a free wi-fi connection for all of our visitors

LOST AND FOUND PROPERTY

We save any items found for one month. Once this time period has passed they are donated to charity

Accessibility

REDUCED MOBILITY

Visitors in wheelchairs will be able to access a large portion of the route followed by visitors, including the most important areas of the home

LIFT

We have a lift with priority usage for persons with disabilities, those with reduced mobility, and pregnant women

TEXT GUIDE + AUGMENTED REALITY

Besides being able to enjoy the Augmented Reality of our SmartGuide, the audio text is also available in Spanish, Catalan, English, French, Italian, German, Chinese, Japanese, Russian and Portuguese.

AUDIO GUIDE + BRAILLE

Our SmartGuide provides a detailed explanation in audio, which is also available in Braille (Catalan, Spanish and English).
